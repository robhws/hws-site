Common concerns

Water issues are not always obvious.

Taste and odor

Chlorine, sulfur smells, metallic taste, or stale-tasting water can make bottled water feel like the easier option.

Hard water buildup

Minerals can leave spots, scale, and residue on fixtures, appliances, dishes, and glassware.

Uncertainty

Many families simply do not know what is in their water or which solution actually fits their home.
