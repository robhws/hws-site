After The Results

Testing is the starting point, not the finish line.

Once you understand your water, the next step is determining whether action is needed and which solution fits your actual conditions.

  • Clean, properly filtered, hydrogen-rich water from a reputable source may mean no additional equipment is needed
  • Known contaminants may require certified filtration or other appropriate treatment
  • Private well results may point toward filtration, additional treatment, or annual monitoring
  • Good source water can support a better premium hydration experience
